# Ray Hernandez - Full Story & Career Timeline

## The Origin
- Grew up in a poor family in Dallas, TX
- Talented artist in high school
- Accepted to top art schools but couldn't afford college
- Got an internship at Rare Medium, a design agency in Dallas

## Career Timeline

### 1999-2000: Rare Medium (Dallas)
- Jr Visual Designer. Internship → full-time
- Made a name as an up-and-coming designer
- Dot-com bubble burst = opportunity. Everyone got laid off and dispersed to other companies, they all reached out to Ray to freelance

### 2000: Stoodio.com Founded (Age 19)
- Started first freelance design agency at 19 years old
- Named it Stoodio.com
- Ran it on and off for 10+ years (2007-2018 on LinkedIn)

### 2001: Match.com & Humankind Studios (Dallas)
- Creative Director from Rare Medium recruited Ray to Match.com
- Worked on innovation, social networking, honed visual design skills
- Also did work at Humankind Studios (6 months overlap)

### 2002-2003: PassionUP (Kailua, Hawaii)
- Headhunted by a startup to build a Hawaii-only dating website
- Site never went live (owner changed his mind)
- This is where Ray fell in love with Hawaii (lived there 3 times total)

### ~2003: NYC Affiliate Marketing Era
- Moved to NYC with friends Chris & Bea
- Learned affiliate marketing from Match.com and PassionUP experience
- THE MYSPACE HACK: Created fake dating profiles using clothed photos from adult affiliate programs, embedded Flash files containing JavaScript pop-unders (MySpace blocked JS but allowed Flash, and Flash can embed JS). Affiliate of FriendFinder.com ($1/click, $30/conversion). Making thousands/month.
- This self-taught growth hacking led to next job

### 2004-2006: Reach Media (Dallas)
- Sr. Visual Designer

### 2005-2007: Wildline → Relevant Searches (Hawaii → SF)
- Company was in affiliate marketing, had Yahoo Paid Inclusion contract
- Promoted porn affiliate sites in search engines, ranking higher than actual brands
- Grew so large they moved to SF (SOMA office) to be closer to Yahoo
- Rebranded from Wildline to Relevant Searches to expand beyond porn industry
- Ray did ALL branding, UI, frontend innovation, tracking systems, and industry blogging

### ~2006: Met His Wife Doe
- Playing guitar on stage for friend Bea's show in SF
- Saw Doe walk through the door
- Doe's dream was always to live in Hawaii

### ~2006-2007: Oakland French Fry Factory
- Moved into 8,000 sq ft old french fry factory in Oakland, CA
- Converted half into massage spa (Doe was a massage therapist)
- Other half was their loft home
- Rented it out after a year, rent covered bills for Hawaii move

### 2007-2009: Hawaii #3 + MyBabyOurBaby
- Moved to Hawaii with Doe (engaged)
- Built MyBabyOurBaby.com - shared photo network for children
- Built on WordPress MU (cutting edge at the time)
- Partner: Beau Lebens (now Artistic Director/Lead, WooCommerce at Automattic)
- Got Mashable coverage on launch day: https://mashable.com/archive/mybabyourbaby
- Won design awards
- ~1K users but gave away too much for free, never monetized
- Shut down - "wasn't a failure, we built something people wanted, just didn't know how to make money"

### 2009-2011: SocialFly Photo Booths (Austin → Dallas)
- Co-founded with Doe after seeing people jam into tiny photo booths
- Designed larger booth ("couch in a box")
- Built custom touchscreen software with PHOTO FILTERS BEFORE INSTAGRAM EXISTED
- Strategy: be the MOST EXPENSIVE on the market ($1,200 for 3 hours)
- Grew from 1 to 6 booths, pre-booked 6 months in advance
- Moved to Dallas to handle north Texas events

### 2011: LikeUs Network Founded (with Steven Ray)
- ORIGIN: Jogging at Katy Trail, sprinted to vending machine, forgot wallet
- "Why can't I just login to Facebook and let them spam me for a free drink?"
- Bought a vending machine + Arduino book, hacked it in Ray's apartment
- Called it "Social Currency"

### THE MARK CUBAN STORY
- Played basketball daily at Lifetime Fitness with Cuban
- Day before moving, decided to pitch him
- Played great that morning, game ended, chickened out
- Followed Cuban to treadmill area to work up courage
- Another player's pacemaker went off, guy flew off treadmill into wall
- Ambulance, chaos, everyone cleared out
- Just Ray and Cuban standing there
- "Can I be a dick and ask you a business question in the middle of all this?"
- Cuban: "Here's my email, I promise I'll read it, I don't promise I'll respond"
- Cuban walks away, gets halfway to door, TURNS AROUND and comes back
