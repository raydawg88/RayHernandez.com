# I miss my family

![](http://photos3.flickr.com/2665605_19f24020a2_m.jpg)

You know...i come from a pretty big family.  A million cousins...tons of uncles and aunts...but my real family are these two people that you see in this photo.  My best friends.  Bea and Chris.  I can't go through a day and not see something or hear something that totally reminds me of them...or some random crazy story about one of our adventures.Like right now...Kate and Leapold is on TBS...yeah yeah...i know i'm watching a sappy movie...but that's besides the point.  They just walked through a block...that bea set up shop and started playing her music for money.  Me and Chris...pretended to be by standers and would toss a few bucks in her guitar case...to get the crowd going.  We ended up meeting a guy who said he was a producer and had produced Everlast's last album.Another time me and chris were crazy drunk at that same corner...it was probably around 5 am and we were totally lost.  so we just said...LEFT!  turned left and went that direction...hoping it would get us home to jersey city.  and an hour later...we stumbled up the stairs to our apartment.Now i'm 3000 miles away in hawaii...chris is back home in texas...and bea is still in new york.  My family is scattered.  I miss them...but nothing can stop me from thinking about them.  I love you guys.  meet me at rudy's.lateray
