# My NewYork Bed

![](http://www.flickr.com/photos/891127_6ce2bf1fef_m.jpg)

as uncomfortable...hot...and too short of a bed this couch was...i really miss it.  carla told me today that she ran into bea and guav at the park.  i thought that was so cute.  they really have something special.  that's what i want.  someone i can do anything with and have a good time.  they rarely fight...and when they do it's one of those over reacting in your head...when nothing really happen kind of things...that only last a day or two.it's pretty hard to think...i know exactly what i want.  i could draw you a picture...give you detailed notes on what i like and dislike...and i'm starting to wonder if my standards or wants...are too high.  i've always been a fan of reach for the stars...or don't sell yourself short...but you get to the point...where you're just a lil lonely.  sucks.makes me think of sex and the city when charlotte gets so upset that she's not married yet.  i think she's my perfect woman.  i always thought i'd be married and have a kid on the way already.  i mean i'm only 23 but i've always wanted to be a young dad.  my dad had me at 19 and i grew up watching him play baseball and race cars and stuff like that...and it was awesome.  i want that with my kid...i want to play and jump and run...and be a kid with my kid.  i think it would be awesome.  i mean i've worked my ass off to be ahead of the game work-wise.  most people my age are just graduating college...but i'm already an art director.  whatever.anyway...don't know what the point of this was...or how i got off subject.  but i saw that photo of that green couch that i lifted up 2 flights of stairs...that would make my back hurt every morning...and really began to miss new york.  i miss bea and guav.  they made my stay unforgetable.  well chris did too of course...since me and him got drunk everynight for 3 months straight...but you know what i mean.  so yeah.  bea and guav...great friends...a great couple...and just great people.  one day i'll have that.  i hope.ray.
