# Birthday's Suck

![](http://photos3.flickr.com/2646588_34aa6e2914_m.jpg)

Well...today is my birthday...thank you to all the people that called or emailed...i appreciate it.  But...there was one thing that i expected...that i expect every year for that matter...that didn't happen.  My Grandma for the past 23 years...has called me crack a dawn...and as soon as i pick up the phone she's already singing "happy birthday to you...happy"...you get the picture (in her lil mescan accent). then she'd always tell me the story about how she'd sing me a lullaby in spanish to get me to go to sleep and rub my ears.  every year.  i loved it...could always count on that.And this morning...crack of dawn...the phone was rining.  so i got a lil grin on my face ready to hear it.  but this time...she just happy birthday...i miss you...i love you.  which is sweet and nice and all that...but where was my song?  i instantly wanted to cry...not cause she didn't sing...but because i instantly felt old.  like it wasn't appropriate for things like that.  i don't think i'm old...or want to feel old...but that's how i felt at that moment.So now i know.  24 years old.  too old for a song.  :(  i still love ya grandma...but i missed not havin it.lateray
