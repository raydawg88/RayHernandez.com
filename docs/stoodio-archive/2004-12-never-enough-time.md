# Never enough time

![](http://photos1.flickr.com/2646950_13620bff13_m.jpg)

I just got home from 2 weeks back home in texas...and i feel like crap.  not like hung over or sick crap...but like i upset a lot of people or just didn't get to see a lot of people that i really wanted to see.  i feel so bad.  i had so many things i wanted to do...and i really didn't get to do barely any of them.  there were a ton of family i didn't get to see...and a ton of friends...that really mean a lot to me that i didn't get to see.  i'm sorry.  i wish i could have.  you should come see me.  anyone...everyone.really i guess this is an apology.  i had a good time these last two weeks.  drank a lot...way too much (thanks chris) and enjoyed my time while i was there.  but there's "never enough time"  to get everything i want done.guess i just have to come back again soon.well...hope you all had a good holiday.ray
