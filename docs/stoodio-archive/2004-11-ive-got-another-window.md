# I've got another window

![](http://www.flickr.com/photos/1792494_55d2eefcc9_m.jpg)

I used to live in a loft in downtown dallas.  it was my dream house...concrete floors, 20 foot ceilings, columns...it was perfect.  but the one thing that i loved about this place was my window.  i'd sit out and just watch the cars go by.  and look at the downtown skyline.  it was my thing to do.  my grandma got me started on it.  when i lived with her when i was little.  and it was time to go to bed...i'd lay on her bed and watch the cars go up and down our street as she sang me a spanish lullaby.  there was this dip in the road right by our house...so people would come flying down the road hit...and sparks would go flying.  it made my day.  i loved it.so having it again makes me so happy.  i watch people play golf during the day...and it makes me want to improve my golf game.  and then at night i see the light go up the mountains and watch all the cars drive by the canal.  it's awesome.  i haven't been this happy in a long time.  thanks window.lateray
