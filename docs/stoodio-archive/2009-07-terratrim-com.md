# TerraTrim.com

![Sample](http://www.stoodio.com/images/terratrim1.png)

TerraTrim.com is electrical analytics company.  What they do is install a box on your electrical box in your house or apartment, and it analyzes how much electricity each appliance in your house is using.  From there it can tell you how much you’re saving, wasting, and how you can improve your electrical consumption.

![Web Design](http://www.stoodio.com/images/terratrim2.png)

They came to me asking for a very modern, clean, but still very vibrant interface.  These are just a couple of the mocks, and this project is still on going.  I’ll post more as the project progresses.
