# Why No-Name Movies are better

last night...i couldn't sleep...like i just laid there on the couch...yes i said couch...i guess i should add that in there. me and my friend chris just moved to new york...kinda...new jersey. and we're staying with one of my best friends bea ( www.onlymebea.com ) and so yeah...here we are. back to the story. well. i'm just layin there last night. thinking about how i haven't really been in a relationship in a pretty long time. well...long time for me anyway. it's been almost 2 years. and i know most people are like whatever that's not a long time...but i'm always in long term relationships. i always have been. i'm jerry mcguire. anyway...the last 2 places i've been...hawaii...where i was this time last year...and now new york...that i just got to...the ladies don't seem to impressed with me. and it's not like i'm the most attractive in shape guy in the world...but i've always been this way...and i've always had some girls after me. now that i'm writing this it sounds so shallow. but i'm not trying to be that way. i mean, it's just that i feel like no one finds me attractive anymore. but i was talking to a friend today. she gave me a great analogy. she told me...you know how a movie that has all the hype will make lots of money even that it's not a great movie. and the movie that didn't get any hype can turn out to be the best movie you've ever seen. that's how i should look at relationships. just cause i don't have a shitload of women after me...and i don't have all the hype. that someone will see me...and i'll be the best thing they've ever seen. :) so yeah...no clue what this whole thing was about...but it's been on my mind. and so i felt like writing it. i'm gonna try to write in this regular...just because i need something to do. raystoodio.com
