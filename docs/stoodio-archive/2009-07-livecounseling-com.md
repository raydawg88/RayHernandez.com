# LiveCounseling.com

![Sample](http://www.stoodio.com/images/livecounseling1.png)

LiveCounseling.comcontacted me in need of some usability help.  They have a wonderful product, but their design needed a little bit of work.  We started slow with a rework of the page with their current design, and that quickly turned into a “lets do a full redesign”.  Above is one of the first redesign mocks of the project.

Since Live Counseling is a up and running company, we had to put the redesign on hold and bust out some new products.  One of the first was afree chat interface.
