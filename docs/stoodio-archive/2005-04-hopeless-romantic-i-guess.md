# Hopeless Romantic.....I guess

Waiting for You......Originally uploaded byDxb Angel.See I've known this for a long time now...but that still doesn't mean i understand it.  So you don't keep sitting there going what the hell is this retard talking about...i just got done reading this journal entry fromGirly Underwear.  Here's  a lil recap:Being aggressive has its advantages. I've never had the pleasure of being rejected often because I don't normally make the first move. But it does feel nice when you go for it and there are rewards. However, being forward also sucks because 1) you might get bored once you reach your goal and 2) it might mean that they aren't as into you as you are them.Girls love, and I mean fucking love guys who are unattainable. I'm not talking about guys who are taken. I'm talking about the cute boys that never show interest in girls or who never try to get girls. You ask their friends what's up and they'll tell you "Oh Ryan? He doesn't date anyone." And they aren't gay. It's the total opposite end of the spectrum from the sleazy player type (one whos got game and hits on everything). The appeal of course is to to be THE ONE to change his mind, but half of the time it's a waste of effort because he's got issues as you will later find out.So here it is:See i really just don't get how this works.  i mean...there's a guy that has never shown interest in you...and even after you hit on him...still doesn't.  and lets even say that he was kinda rude in ignoring you.  this...actually makes him hotter.  :: shakes head::  i mean...seriously...i thought i understood a lot about women.  i mean i grew up in a beauty salon listening to women bitch about every man they've ever been with.  but this one...which is a very important one apparently...just doesn't stick.Maybe it's that i'm a romantic.  i love the romantic movies...where a couple see one another in some random place...and instantly they can't take their eyes off of one another...like it was fate.  and how a guy or a girl can do something to sweep someone off their feet...and show someone how much they want to be with that person.  and no...i'm not gay....so get that out of your head.  that kind of romance is what i want...and have wanted for a long time.  the movie life.  but...if what i'm getting from a very beautiful woman's journal...and from every gorgeous woman i have as a friend...then movies are full of shit.  if i have tats...play in a band...or both...or any combination...all you have to do is...walk around...and pretend like you could care less that someone is talking to you.  sounds easy enough.but here's what i think would happen if someone like me attemtps that.  i'd just do my thing...at a bar or a club...and instead of intriging some gorgeous woman...i think they'd just continue to not know i'm even there.  maybe there's a level of good looking-ness that you have to have...before this ideal attraction scheme can work.  not sure.  i mean i've gone to a ton of places and just had a good time...and not even looked up a million times.  so this doesn't work for me.here's my question to all of you beautiful women.  how does a guy...that isn't in a band...or has tats...or even is the most attractive person in the world...get a beautiful woman...to want to come up and meet him.  cause let's face it...it's every guys dream to have beautiful women come up and talk to them.  cause although there are a ton of guys that play the numbers and hit on every girl out there...there is a majority out there...that is too shy...or too scared of rejection to even look in your direction.  just lookin for some help i guess.  hook it uplateray
