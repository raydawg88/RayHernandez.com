# My last painting

![](http://www.flickr.com/photos/1229252_327ee5e3fd_m.jpg)

I used to actually consider myself an artist...but as time goes by...you kinda lose the feeling.  it's been almost a year since i picked up a brush.  i miss it.  it's sad really.  my corporate work and making money has totally taken away from something that i used to enjoy doing.  every week or so i tell myself i'm gonna go get some paints and some canvas...but it never seems to happen.i look at my site all the time...and wonder if i can even pull of some of the stuff that's on there right now.  i watched great expectations last night...which is my ultimate favorite movie...and normally my inspiration movie.  but...this time.  i didn't want to pick up a sketch book and draw...this time...i just went to bed.  maybe i've totally lost that want to have a gallery show...or even to show off my work.  i don't know.  i'm gonna try to get into it again.  maybe it's gone.  we'll see.ray
