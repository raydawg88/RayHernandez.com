# Friends?

![](http://photos1.flickr.com/1229476_b403bc16d9_m.jpg)

Have you ever felt like you have aa awesome friend?  You hang out...you laugh...you always smiling...just have an great time whenever you're with that person.  And then...after months...maybe even years...you realize...that you don't know any of their other friends...you don't ever hang out with that person with other people around.  It's always just you two.  And not in things you'd like to do alone way.Just started thinking about that.  I feel i have quiet a few friends like that.  Makes you think about stupid shit like...you're not good enough to meet other people's friends or family...which i don't think is true...but these types of things just go running through my head i can't help it.   Anyway just curious if i was the only one with this thought.I guess this article got me thinking about it that my friend amber sent to me.  It was about a couple that have been seeing each other for about 2 months...and the guy was being very respectful and not pushing sex on her.  But it never happened...always hang out...go eat dinner...movies...everything...well except sex.  And one day she asks him to help her move out of her apartment...so he does.  they're moving the bed...and there are a shitload of used condom wrappers on the floor.  She says " oh sorry i'll clean that up".  Like it's no big deal at all.  He's like what the fuck is this.  She's like what we're not exclusive...he's like i could care less if we're exclusive...you're not having sex with me.  She's like it's more than just physical with you...i have a great time.  i love being with you.  So basically the guy comes up with a 2 date rule.  if you go out with someone more than twice and nothing happens.  move on.  maybe that's how it should be.  Wish i could be mean enough to do that.lateray
