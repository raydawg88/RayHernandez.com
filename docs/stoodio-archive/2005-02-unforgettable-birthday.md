# Unforgettable Birthday

![](http://photos2.flickr.com/3586283_9189ecdd1e_m.jpg)

Well it started off pretty normal.  Went to a really nice dinner with my company...had some good food...good conversations...got some really cool paintings as a gift.  it was nice.  my boss and his wife had a few drinks...but for some reason i just felt like coke.  so me and my trusty refreshing friend just chilled together while i ate my steak.the night starts to come to an end...and it's my birthday...so now it's time to party.  so we pay the bill...head to the cars and head for the bar.  Being the little girl that i am...i asked beau to run me by the house so i can change shirts.  so we're on the highway...we pull of to turn on his street...or boss comes behind us...passes us....then....BAAAAAAAAAAAAAAMMMMMMMMMMMMMMM!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!they run straight into this dark green nissan truck.  t boned them...spinning that truck around and sending themselves into a ditch.  it was a total movie scene...with a nice round explosion of parts and glass.  we ran over there...and our boss and wife some how were fine.  they guy in the truck was passed out for about 10 minutes though.  but it all worked out.  everybody left okay...but damn.  happy birthday.so after that...i didn't even feel like drinking...we went i had one drink then said fuck it...and went back to the hotel.scary thing is my boss wasn't wearing a seat belt and ended up breaking the windshield with the back of his head.  but...this is a moment of silence for the vehicles lost today...or last night...............................................................................k...well...good times.lateray
