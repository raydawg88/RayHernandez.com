# Only I could bitch about paradise

k...from dallas...big ass city.  lived on oahu...big ass city...last year.  just moved to maui from new york...biggest ass city there is.  i need the city.  it's not like i need it like i need ice cream no.  i mean i really need it to survive.  i understand why people that are stuck in places they don't like fall into this depression or kill themselves or something.  k...now don't get me wrong.  i realize that i'm on a tropical island...and there's a million people that would give their left testicle to live here...but...and a big but....it's different when you're the only person on the island.  no friends...no family nothing.  just a job...and a beach that you don't get to see cause you're working most of the time.had this last year in oahu...but there was so much to do there...and after about 6 months i had friends...some friends that i love dearly.  and i'm at that point again.  i'm stuck on an island with no friends....sept beau...so don't freak out bitch if you read this.  but even still...he's australian....and you know how we all hate australians.  anyway.  so yeah...back to me bitching about paradise...right...cause that's how everyone else see's it.  i guess i just miss my family and my friends...and as weird as it sounds i miss my parents.  they're going through a divorce so things are weird...but i'm an only child....and it's always just been me and them...through everything...and now they live in separate houses...and i'm 5 thousand miles away.  it's just weird.anyway...i just needed to vent....i move back to oahu december first so that will make my year.  except for the fact that i may lose my job.  so it may be a good but bad breakup.  we'll see.  anyway...thanks for listening...or just sitting there...or for doing nothing what so ever...cause no one reads this...but yeah.  two fingers...i'm outray
