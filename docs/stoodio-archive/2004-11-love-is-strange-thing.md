# Love is a strange thing...

![](http://www.flickr.com/photos/1204107_e09451c244_m.jpg)

I mean love is of course a strange thing...but i'm talking about a different kind of love.  like a family love.  this picture is my cousin.  my aunts have been trying to adopt him for a really long time now.  they live in florida and i've never even met jonah...my cousin.  but everytime i see a photo of him...he just makes me smile.  i love this kid.  he's awesome.  i can't wait til i actually get to play with him.i'm just saying...it's weird...how you can love someone...without ever actually meeting them.  and i don't mean that in a pyscho i love sandra bullock kinda way.  i mean it like this.  jonah is the cutest kid i've ever seen...and i can't wait to take him around the mall and have all the ladies come up to me and tell me how cute my son is.  i love him.ray
