# Uplifting Day

the ZapperOriginally uploaded byraydawg88.I know this is going to sound crazy, but Nintendo has made my week...no lets say month...totally better.  Let me back up.  Yesterday my friendshanehad to move in with me.  He hasn't found a place to live yet and had to be out of his current apartment.  So all of his stuff is now in what used to be my very pretty apartment.I was getting a little overwhelmed...when i started going through one of his boxes.  It was then that a orange glow hit me like god slamming lightning down on someone damning his name.  The orange came from the Nintendo gun...the zapper.  An instant smile came onto my face.  As i picked it up it unveiled another piece.  The all mighty powerglove.  The one thing i wanted so bad when i was a little kid.  It was one after the other.  Next came a ton of games...and all of my favorites.  Then came sega genesis games.  Altered Beast, Gauntlet, Street Fighter Turbo.  It was beautiful.My whole month of dealing with crap from work, family, women...all disappeared with a few pieces of my childhood.  It was awesome.  So thanks Shane for making my apartment messy...and making my day.ray
