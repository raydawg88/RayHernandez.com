# The Toph v.1

![Sample](http://farm2.static.flickr.com/1220/1056133061_2a4884f6b0_o.jpg)

Who is Toph? He speaks loudly, about everything. He was trying to find a blog that included everything from hot chicks, sports, entertainment, celeb gossip, sex tapes, and everything about himself. Believe it or not this blog did not exist. So, I made it happen. It’s pretty much what I do on a daily basis. I’m fucking Toph, what more do you expect?

Toph came to me looking for a design for his blog, that told a story about who was, and what he is all about.  The term “Gangsta” came up about 100 times in the conversation, so I thought what better way to portray him.  I’d make it dark and “Gangsta”.  This was the first mock of many, and this project is still in the works.
