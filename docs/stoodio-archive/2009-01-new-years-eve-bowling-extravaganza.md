# New Years Eve Bowling Extravaganza

![Sample](http://farm4.static.flickr.com/3100/3161106379_486bdd3ed9_m.jpg)

What do you do when you’re not gonna drink at new years?  Well you bowl…of course. This year nobody wanted to drink, so we put ourself on a 2 and a half hour long waiting list to get a bowling lane at 300 lanes in addison.  First let me say that 300 lanes is the most amazing bowling alley I’ve ever seen.  You walk into a pool hall, go through the fancy restaurant with lounge couches and chairs, and then make your way to the black-lit bowling lanes.  It’s half club half bowling alley.  good stuff.

So since the wait was so long, we only got to play 2 games.  But it was still a good time.  Not as good as some people…Amber…but we can’t all be as amazing as her.  Below is a few pictures and a few videos.  Enjoy.
