# Gurutoy.com - Visual Search Engine

![Sample](http://farm4.static.flickr.com/3096/3083146433_743b6b4361_o.png)

Gurutoy was an idea that Brad Suzuki had 3 years ago.  A way to give people a catalyst to find new and creative information visually.Brad had a tough time finding someone who could turn his vision into a reality.  This was until he met me.  After a month and a half of design and development Gurutoy.com is now live.  It has a clean yet vibrant interface, which puts the emphasis on the photos. The content is people powered, allowing users to have the say in what photos/visuals represents the sites they promote.

![Gurutoy.com](http://farm4.static.flickr.com/3213/3083984790_061da364c2_o.png)

Gurutoy.com

About GurutoyThe concept of Gurutoy emerged in December 2005. We wanted to construct a more organized way of searching that was fresh and new.The idea was to create a virtual art gallery of website links that would be searchable thru keywords and phrases. All content will be uploaded by users. You tell us what you like out there on the internet. Another intention was to promote creativity, uploaded pictures can be anything deemed meaningful for attracting someone to click on to the site link.

Become a member and search other user’s favorite websites that they’ve uploaded. With Gurutoy, you can discover websites you never knew existed or reference back to your favorites. Gurutoy is meant to be a fun, easy way to search for websites, powered by the people to give search back to the people.
