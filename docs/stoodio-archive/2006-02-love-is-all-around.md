# Love is all around…

![Sample](http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2323/1511438800_9b06928ae7.jpg?v=0)

So my really good friend…Amber…got engaged this weekend.You can read all about it here.  It’s really great.  The really do have a love at first sight story.  Allow me to break it down for you in 10 seconds.  Meet at bar….Drink at bar…Puke in bar…Makeout.  The end.  Been together ever since.  I love them.

Anyway along with that great news…I got some of my own.  Last night I was telling Doan about Amber and Tyler…oh yeah…that’s their names.  Sorry…forgot to add that.  Anyway…I was telling her all about it and she jumps up and says Oh yeah!  I have something for you.  I was like ummm okay.  So I followed her into the room.  She pulls out this little pink asian zipper bag.  I blink and when I open up my eyes…she’s down on one knee.  I was like WTF?  And she goes “Ray…will you be my…(hands me the ring)….MY LIFELONG SCREW?”

I about shit myself I was laughing so hard.  It was so awesome.  My girl really is as random as I am.I took some pictures so you guys can see.

![](http://farm1.static.flickr.com/42/103515635_ce2afda949.jpg?v=0)

I love random love stories.
